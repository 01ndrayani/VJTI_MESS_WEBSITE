MESS RULES

MESS SCHEDULE

WEEKDAY WEEKEND
BREAKFAST 7:30am to 9:00am 8:00am to 9:00am
LUNCH 12:30pm to 2:00pm 12:30pm to 2:00pm
DINNER 7:30pm to 9:00pm 7:30pm to 9:00pm

  • Timings and Attendance: All girls residing in the hostel are expected to adhere to the designated mess timings strictly. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner timings will be clearly communicated, and it is essential for students to be punctual. Regular attendance at meals is required, and prior intimation must be given to the hostel authorities in case of any unavoidable absence.

  • Hygiene and Cleanliness: Maintaining cleanliness and hygiene in the mess area is of utmost importance. Girls students are expected to clear their trays and dishes after meals and ensure that the dining hall and surrounding areas are clean and tidy. Waste segregation guidelines must be followed, and any spills or mess should be promptly reported to the mess staff.

  • Respectful Conduct: The mess is a shared space, and it is crucial to maintain a respectful and cordial atmosphere. Any form of discrimination, harassment, or unruly behavior will not be tolerated. Students are encouraged to engage in friendly and inclusive interactions with their fellow peers, fostering a sense of unity and mutual respect.

At VJTI, we believe that adherence to these mess rules will not only facilitate a smooth dining experience but also cultivate a sense of responsibility and discipline among our female students, fostering an environment conducive to academic growth and personal development.
